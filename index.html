Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a meal?

Tap the Snaps tab at the bottom of the app, then tap the camera icon. Point your camera at your meal and NutriScan's AI will recognize every ingredient and compute portions in seconds.

How do I scan a nutrition label?

Tap the Label Scan tab at the bottom of the app. Then tap "Take a Photo or Select Image" and point your camera at any nutrition facts label on a grocery or supplement item.

Is NutriScan free?

Yes — NutriScan is completely free with no subscriptions or paywalls. It was built by students at Purdue University who believe nutrition technology should work for everyone.

How do I update my nutrition goals?

Tap "My Profile" in the top right corner of the home screen to update your dietary preferences, calorie targets, and macro goals.

The app crashed or isn't working — what do I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure your iOS is up to date, then contact us at the email below and we'll help you resolve it.
