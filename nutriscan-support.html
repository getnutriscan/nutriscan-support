Is NutriScan free?

Yes — NutriScan is completely free with no subscriptions or paywalls.

How does food scanning work?

Point your camera at any food item and NutriScan's AI will identify it and return detailed nutrition facts powered by the USDA Food Data Central database.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your personal profile and preferences are stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I find local food banks?

Set up your profile with your location and dietary preferences, and NutriScan will surface nearby food banks and free healthy food resources automatically.
